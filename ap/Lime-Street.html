THE NEW BUILDING
AT LIME STREET

London, UK

This building on the edge of the Leadenhall Conservation Area in the City of London provides office accommodation on the upper floors with restaurant and shop space at ground and basement level. It contains the Leadenhall market area by providing a colonnaded arcade on the south edge of the market.
